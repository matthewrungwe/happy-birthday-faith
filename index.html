Happy 20th Birthday.

This year is a as much a milestone as your 21st. You have come a long way and you have endured a lot. Today as any other day it marks the birth of a new you and a better you. Life going forwards is just going to be life. It is going to be filled happy, sad, fun, painful, joyful and challenging moments. At least this time you would have a mutual and better perspective.

You are a strong woman and you are only going to get stronger. Remember that you are amazing and you are a force to be reckoned with. May God continue to watch over you and bless you in all you pray for and all you do.

What to expect going forward?

What's going to become apparent over the years is that your faith and your decisions will become very important. You will find yourself in the deep end a few times. To make better decisions, know your heart can deceive you. Pay attention to red flags and don't do what doesn't feel right in your heart. Ask yourself what story you want to tell tomorrow and make a decisions that will bring smiles. Do the right thing always and don't wait until it's too late to change. Do everything with love.

Live your life with faith, stand for what you believe in and have hope for what you do not yet see. Your faith will be the only thing that can hold you through the tide.
